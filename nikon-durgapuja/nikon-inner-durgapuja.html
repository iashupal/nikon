DURGA PUJA

Durga Puja, an annual Hindu festival is believed to be the greatest festival of Bengali people. The multi-day festival that features elaborate temple and pandals, performance arts and more, marks the celebration of victory of good over evil. Held over a five-day period as a tradition, married daughters revisit their parents and celebrate the Durga Puja together, a symbolism for goodies Durga who is believed to return to her parent's home for the festival.
